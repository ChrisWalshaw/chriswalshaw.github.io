TuneGraph Demonstrator

These pages demonstrate the use of collection-based TuneGraphs - that is TuneGraphs which just reflect melodic similarities within a tune collection (rather than within the entire abc corpus).

The aim is to show what is possible so that owners of abc tune collections can deploy collection-based TuneGraphs on their own sites.

There are two variants:

  • single tunes per page (slightly easier to deploy)
  • multiple tunes per page (slightly harder to deploy but more flexible)

The TuneGraph demonstrator also allow users to explore similarities in the English morris dance canon.
